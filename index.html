A blockchain game with a fish theme

Crypto Fishing is an NFT play-to-earn game with a fish theme, in which you can perform multiple tasks with them such as raising, caring, battles and other tasks.

NFT PORTFOLIO

Resize the browser window to see the responsive effect.

Common Egg

0.091 BNB

The first of three eggs, a common egg ensures you a common rarity fish that is the lowest rarity.

Epic Egg

0.182 BNB

The second of three eggs. An epic egg guarantees you a fish of epic rarity which is the intermediate rarity.

Legendary Egg

0.273 BNB

The third of three eggs, a legendary egg ensures you a fish of legendary rarity which is the highest rarity that exists in the world of Crypto Fishing.

Fish tank

0.821 BNB

An empty fish tank to raise your fish.
