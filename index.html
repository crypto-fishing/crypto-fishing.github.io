Common Egg

0.01 BNB

The first of three eggs, a common egg ensures you a common rarity fish that is the lowest rarity.

Epic Egg

0.02 BNB

The second of three eggs. An epic egg guarantees you a fish of epic rarity which is the intermediate rarity.

Legendary Egg

0.03 BNB

The third of three eggs, a legendary egg ensures you a fish of legendary rarity which is the highest rarity that exists in the world of Crypto Fishing.
